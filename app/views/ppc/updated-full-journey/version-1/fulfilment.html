How can we send you your prepayment certificate details?

Information:

We do not send out plastic cards any more because they take time to reach you. They are also costly to the NHS and the environment.

We can send your prepayment certificate straight away by email. Or, you can print it at the end of the service.

You will get a:

  • certificate number
  • start date
  • end date

Your certificate will automatically renew after 12 months, as long as you don't cancel your Direct Debit.

We'll send your new certificate by email, and a reminder one month before your renewal date.
