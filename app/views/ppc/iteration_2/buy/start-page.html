Buy an NHS Prescription Prepayment Certificate

Use this service to save money on your NHS prescriptions.

A prescription costs £9.15 per item. You can prepay by credit or debit card, or by Direct Debit, for an unlimited number of prescriptions.

A prescription prepayment certificate costs:

  • £29.65 for 3 months
  • £105.90 for 12 months (or 10 Direct Debit instalments of £10.59)

You'll need:

  • your personal details, including name, date of birth and address
  • bank or payment card details so that we can process your payment

We'll also ask for your NHS number, if you have it.

Once you have your certificate, you'll be able to collect prescriptions straight away, if your start date is today or earlier.

You may have to show your certificate details when you collect your prescription.

Buying a prescription prepayment certificate takes around 10 minutes.
