What option is best for me?

12 months

The 12-month option costs £105.90 and will save you money if you need more than 11 prescribed items in a year.

If you choose to pay this by Direct Debit, your prescription prepayment certificate will automatically renew after 12 months. You can choose to cancel this if you want.

3 months

The 3-month option costs £29.65 and will save you money if you need more than 3 prescribed items in 3 months.

This option is could be convenient to suit a temporary situation where several medicines may be needed.
