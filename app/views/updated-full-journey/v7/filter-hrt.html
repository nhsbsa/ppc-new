You may save more with an HRT PPC

Depending on how may of your prescriptions are for HRT medication you may save more buying an HRT PPC and paying for other prescriptions separately.

HRT PPCs cover certain HRT medicines for a set price of £19.80 (the same price as 2 prescribed items) and is valid for 12 months.

For example, if you get 4 items in a 3-month period and 3 of the items are HRT medicines covered by the HRT PPC. It is cheaper to buy an HRT PPC for £19.90 and pay the prescription charge of £9.90 for the other item. This costs £29.70 in total so it is cheaper than buying a 3 month PPC for £32.05.

Information:

If you have a PPC that covers all NHS prescriptions you do not need to buy an HRT PPC.
