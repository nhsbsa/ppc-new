Buy an NHS Prescription Prepayment Certificate (PPC)

Important: Important information

If you pay for NHS prescribed hormone replacement therapy (HRT) medicine, then an HRT PPC could save you more money than a PPC (opens in a new tab).

Use this service to buy a PPC.

A PPC allows you to save money on NHS prescriptions, including dental prescriptions, by prepaying for them no matter how many you need.

You can pay a set price for prescriptions for 3 or 12 months, no matter how many you need.

A prescription costs £9.65 per item, but prepaying for an unlimited number of prescriptions is:

  • £31.25 for 3 months
  • £111.60 for 12 months (or 10 Direct Debit instalments of £11.16)

How it works

Show your certificate details when you collect your prescription. You can collect prescriptions straight away, if your start date is today or earlier.

We can send your certificate details by email, or you can print them at the end of the service.

If you are applying on behalf of someone else, fill in the application using their details rather than your own.

Before you start

You'll need:

  • a valid debit or credit card to buy a PPC

Other ways to buy

The quickest way to buy a certificate is online. If you can't, there are other ways to buy one.

You cannot get a PPC by post using this online service. If you need it by post, you can buy one from some pharmacies.
