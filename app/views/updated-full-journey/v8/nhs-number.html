You do not need to provide your NHS number, but it can help us match your details correctly.

Where to find your NHS number

If you're registered with a GP, you will have a 10 digit NHS number unique to you.

You can find it on the NHS app or use our online NHS number finder (opens in new tab).

It is also on any letters or documents you receive from the NHS, such as:

  • prescriptions
  • test results
  • appointment letters
  • maternity notes
