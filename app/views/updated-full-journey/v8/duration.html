Information:

If you're aged 58 or 59

Your prepayment certificate will not automatically renew because you're approaching age 60 - at which point you will be eligible for free prescriptions.

Your first installment will be taken on 15 August 2023. After that you can contact us to change the payment date.

See our Knowledge Base (opens in new tab) for more information on Direct Debit renewals.

What option is best for me?

It's important you carefully consider which certificate is best for you, based on your individual circumstances.

A 3-month certificate costs £32.05, which will save you money if you need more than 3 items in 3 months.

A 12-month certificate costs £114.50, which will save you money if you need more than 11 items in 12 months.

Depending on how many prescription items you need and for how long you might need them, it may be cheaper if you purchase one or more 3-month prepayment certificates.
