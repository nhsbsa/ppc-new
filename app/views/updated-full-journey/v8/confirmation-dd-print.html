Collecting prescriptions

Tick box 'F' on the back of your prescription.

Pharmacy staff ask for prepayment details. If you do not have them with you, they tick 'Evidence not seen' on the prescription. They will not charge you.

We prevent fraud by matching certificate details on our database with prescriptions.
